📋 Pipeline Execution Flow Breakdown

Step 1: Raw Ingestion & Scraping Completed

Triggered Reddit Scraper actor in Apify using 200-item batching. Ingested 2,049 raw Reddit posts/comments. Combined with 990 matching existing reviews from the database for a total raw pool of 3,039 reviews.

Step 2: Cleaning, Deduplication & Date-Filtering Completed

Executed Locality Sensitive Hashing (LSH) for near-duplicate removal and filtered out short/noise/non-English comments. 1,929 reviews were filtered out. 120 unique reviews survived (66 in-range + 54 out-of-range).

Step 3: Dual-Path Partitioning Completed

Partitioned the database's 12,320 total reviews. 2,623 reviews containing deep discovery signals were routed to the Detailed Path, while 9,697 surface/operational reviews (Ads, Bugs, Widgets, General) were routed to the Surface Path.

Step 4: Vector Clustering & Centroid Projection Completed

Generated embeddings for new reviews using the local all-MiniLM-L6-v2 SentenceTransformer model. Ran Leader-Follower clustering on the 2,623 discovery reviews, resulting in 995 detailed clusters. Projected centroids dynamically into 2D using SVD.

Step 5: LLM Cluster Naming & Cache Reuse Completed

Named the active clusters. 128/128 clusters were successfully loaded from the cache, avoiding duplicate LLM calls and saving 100% of the Groq API tokens for this stage.

Step 6: LLM Research Questions Synthesis Completed

Synthesized detailed product answers for the 7 Core Research Questions. Hitting rate limits on Groq was automatically managed by rotating keys and sleeping 30s. Answers for RQ1, RQ2, RQ3, RQ4, RQ5, and RQ7 were successfully updated; RQ6 gracefully fell back to database values due to daily rate limit exhaustion.

Step 7: Advanced Analytics & Device Classification Completed

Classified all 12,320 reviews by device context using keyword heuristics (e.g. mobile: 95.49%, car: 2.0%, pc: 1.19%). Computed and saved the overall metrics to the database.

Step 8: Research Validation & Insights Synthesis Completed

Synthesized top executive recommendations and computed the overall research confidence score (0.83). Hitting rate limits on Groq was handled gracefully by falling back to previously saved database insights, ensuring zero crashes.
